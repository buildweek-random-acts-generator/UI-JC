How it Works

Inspiration

People want to do good and serve others, but with our busy lives it can be hard to remember to do it. Our app can solve this problem.

Empowerment

RandomActs generator will randomly choose a person from your contacts, and give you suggestions for ways to serve them.

Improvement

Using this app is a great way to remember be of service, and an even better way to inspire others to spread more kindness as well!
